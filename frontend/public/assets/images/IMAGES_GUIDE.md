# Images Guide — Drop your images here

## Hero Banner
- **File:** `hero-banner.jpg`  
- **Path:** `public/assets/images/hero-banner.jpg`
- **Usage:** Background of the "Your favorite customized Club Jerseys" section
- **Recommended size:** 1440 × 400px or wider

## Product Images
Product images are **seeded via the Django admin or seed command**.
When you run `python manage.py seed_data` and upload images through the admin at
`/admin`, they will appear automatically in the product cards and detail pages.
